NARENDRA MODI

Narendra Damodardas Modi born on 17 September 1950, is an Indian politician serving as the 14th and current prime minister of India since May 2014. Modi was the chief minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the Member of Parliament from Varanasi.
